Timetable

DoBu Martial Arts offers a structured timetable catering to various needs. Kids' classes are scheduled from 3 PM to 5 PM, providing a focused environment for young martial artists. For those looking for extra practice, open mat sessions are available from 1 PM to 2:30 PM, allowing students to refine their skills. Additionally, private tuition is offered from 10:30 AM to 12 PM, ensuring personalized instruction. Regular classes occur throughout the day, filling the gaps between these specific sessions to accommodate all skill levels and schedules.
