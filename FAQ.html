Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

Do i Need Prior Experiance?

No prior experience is required. DoBu Martial Arts welcomes individuals of all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners

What martial arts disciplines are offered?

DoBu Martial Arts teaches a variety of martial arts including boxing, judo, Mauy Thai, taekwondo, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ), and karate

What age groups do you cater to?

Classes are available for both children and adults. The curriculum is designed to accommodate various age groups and skill levels

Classes and Training

What should I wear for my first class?

New students are advised to wear comfortable clothing such as a loose-fitting t-shirt and tracksuit bottoms. Specific uniforms or gear will be provided by the dojo as needed

Are classes suitable for all fitness levels?

Yes, classes are designed to improve fitness regardless of your current level. The instructors will help you enhance your strength, flexibility, and overall health

Membership and Fees

Is there a membership fee?

Yes, there are membership options available that may include access to special classes and events. Specific pricing details can be obtained by found on our in the prices section of the website

What equipment do I need?

All necessary equipment is provided by DoBu Martial Arts, so students do not need to invest in expensive gear before starting their trainings

Safety and Support
