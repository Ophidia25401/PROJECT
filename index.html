About Dobu

Welcome to DOBU Martial Arts, situated at Arts, St Georges Avenue, South Shields, , where innovation and tradition coexist. DOBU is a vibrant martial arts organisation that was founded in 2022 and is committed to empowering people with self-confidence, strength, and discipline. From novices to seasoned practitioners, our knowledgeable instructors provide specialised training programs in a variety of martial arts genres.

At DOBU , we think martial arts have the ability to change people, not just by increasing physical fitness but also by promoting mental toughness and personal development. DOBU is the best place to begin your adventure if you want to improve your fitness, learn self-defence, or advance your martial arts abilities. Come to South Shields with us and join a caring, enthusiastic community committed to Strengthening the community
